### SMART CONTRACT NETWORKS ###
For transferring ETH, BNB, POL (MATIC), ARB, AVAX

# rpc_url
Obtain your unique endpoint links for different networks from:
**https://developer.metamask.io/key/all-endpoints**
and replace the values for the `rpc_url` fields in the file:
**metadata/networks.json**

# contract_address
Retrieve the contract addresses on various networks
and replace the values for the `contract_address` variables in the file:
**metadata/networks.json**

# abi
If the smart contract structure is **identical** across different networks, their ABIs will be the same.
Therefore, paste your smart contract's ABI into the file located at:
**metadata/abi.json**

### TRX ###
To transfer the TRON cryptocurrency, you must create an account on:
**https://www.trongrid.io**
Save your corresponding **API KEY** in the local environment variables (`.env`) under the key:
**TRON_API_TRONGRID**
Also, save the private key for your TRX wallet in the local variable:
**TRX_WALLET_PK**

* The method for obtaining the private key is detailed in the starred section below.
--------------------------------------------
### BTC, LTC, DOGE, DASH ###
We use the **BlockCypher** service to facilitate the transfer of these cryptocurrencies.
To do this, you must register on:
**https://accounts.blockcypher.com/tokens**
and save your corresponding token in the local environment variables under the key:
**BLOCK_CYPHER_API_TOKEN**

You must also save the following variables (your wallet addresses and corresponding private keys for these coins) in your local environment:
* **BITCOIN_WALLET_PK**
* **BITCOIN_WALLET_ADDRESS**
* **DOGE_WALLET_PK**
* **DOGE_WALLET_ADDRESS**
* **LITECOIN_WALLET_PK**
* **LITECOIN_WALLET_ADDRESS**
* **DASH_WALLET_PK**
* **DASH_WALLET_ADDRESS**

* The method for obtaining the private key is detailed in the starred section below.

### METHOD FOR OBTAINING THE PRIVATE KEY ###

### SOL
For Solana, you must save your wallet's **SEED PHRASE** in the local variable:
**SEED_WALLET**
and your SOL wallet address in the local variable:
**MY_SOL_Wallet_ADDRESS**

Then, use the following function (located in `___utils/service/crypto/utils/FIND_ACCOUNT_SOL.py`) to search for your private key:
`find_my_solana_account(SEED_WALLET, MY_SOL_Wallet_ADDRESS, max_search)`
The private key will then be displayed in the output.

### BTC, BNB, TRX, DOGE, LTC, DASH
To obtain the private key for these cryptocurrencies, you can use an **offline tool** such as:
**bip39-standalone.html**

To do this:
1.  Enter your wallet's **Seed Phrase** into the "**BIP39 Mnemonic**" field.
2.  Select your desired cryptocurrency.
3.  In the "**Derived Addresses**" section, search for the wallet address that matches your target coin.
4.  Extract the **Private Key** corresponding to your address.

**Note:** If you cannot find your address, check the different tabs under the **Derivation Path** section to find the pattern that matches your wallet address.
